FAQ

1.How do i start choosing which plant to buy?

People often make the mistake of choosing which plant they want before they know where it should go. The truth is, you should decide where you want your plant before you decide which plant to buy. This is because different plants will thrive in different spots, depending on the amount of space and light available.
